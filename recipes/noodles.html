Noodles

Description

Noodles are a fast and filing savoury meal. They can be cooked fast, and customized.

I will show you a general way to get one ready.

Ingredients

  • A pot
  • A packet of your favourite noodles
  • 400 ml of water

Steps

  1. Pour water into the pot.
  2. Let the water bowl.
  3. Drop in your noodles.
  4. Let it simmer for 3 minutes.
  5. Make any topping of your choice on the side.
  6. Take your noodles out in a bowl.
  7. Mix in the seasoning packet(s).
  8. Add your topping and enjoy.
