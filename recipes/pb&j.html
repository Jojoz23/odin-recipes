PB&J

Description

A PB&J is a classic and tasty delicacy. It can serve as a breakfast, desert, treat whatever you want!

If you have never tasted it you are missing out. Let me show you how it's done.

Ingredients

  • Two pieces of bread of your choice
  • Your favourite jam
  • Peanut butter

Steps

  1. Toast yourr bread.
  2. Spread your jam on one slice.
  3. Spread the peanut butter on the other slice.
  4. Put the two slices together as a sandwich. Voila enjoy!
